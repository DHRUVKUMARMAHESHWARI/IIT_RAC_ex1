Applications of pressure gauge

Applications of pressure gauge

1. Pressure gauges are used to measure the pressure of liquids, vapors, solids, and gases.

2. It is used for the inspection of air brakes on trucks.

3. Pressure gauges are used in chemical, petrochemical, sanitary, pharmaceutical, and process industries.

4. Pressure gauges are used in HVAC, refrigeration, ventilation, food, and beverage industries

5. They are also used in eliminating the potential leak paths.


Advantages of pressure gauge

1. Readings are easy to read and often appear fast on the screen

2. Some gauges provide international measurements such as bar, psi, MPa, and many others

3. Provides precise measurements

4. No requirement of any operator interpretation

5. No parallax error occurred

6. Back lit capability

7. Provides accurate readings in high vibration
